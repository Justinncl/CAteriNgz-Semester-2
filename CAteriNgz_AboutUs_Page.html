Welcome to CAteriNgz "Good Food Brings Good Mood"


Our Founder

CAteriNgz was founded by Master Chef’s one and only Chef Arnold Purnomo in 2024 with the vision and mission of serving and delivering affordable and quality food to every person in the world. CAteriNgz was first established as an idea imagined by Chef Arnold whom as a kid, was raised in a low economy family thus, he couldn’t afford healthy and nutritious food due to the lack of funds to do so. Eventually, he debuted his career as a renowned chef and created the catering service we now know as CAteriNgz. CAteriNgz is sworn to only use quality products and raw materials in the making of our food products to ensure that the nutrition of every person who eats our food is fulfilled and are able to live long healthy lives. “Good food brings good mood” - Chef Arnold Purnomo

Our Vision & Mission

CAteriNgz envisions to become the leading provider of catering services, recognized for affordability, quality, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Our mission at CAteriNgz is to provide nutritious food at affordable prices using only the finest of raw ingredients and products in order to assure our customers will be satisfied with our quality products. CAteriNgz also aims to achieve the "Top Brand" certification and also to expand branches in other districts to allow others to enjoy and have the taste of our quality products.
